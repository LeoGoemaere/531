Explanations

Your training maxes will be calculated at 90% of your true max.

The general rule of thumb is to use 50-60% of your Training Max for the 5 sets of 10 reps. (The app take 50%)

More infos here.

the first three or four sets warm-up are supersetted with band pull aparts.
The rest of the sets are done with rows, curls or some kind of pull-up between the main lifts (bench press and press).
For the squat and the deadlift, superset extra hip mobility work and static stretching for the hip flexors, chins/pull-ups and some lighter abdominal work.

For the assistances lifts, perform the sets with 50% of your TM, Second month: Perform the sets with 60% of your TM, Third month: Perform the sets with 70% of your training max.

The yellow rows are the joker sets (optional sets).

The AMRAP rows is the First set last variation.
